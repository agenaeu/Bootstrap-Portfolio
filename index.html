About Me


My name is Stephen Dillon Rice, however I've gone by Dillon my entire life. I'm 28 years old and have recently finished my bachelors degree in general biology. I wanted to pursue a career in the medical field but quickly realized that I could not afford to go, nor could I likely get in. Instead I turned my attention towards programming. I've dabbled with programming in the past and thought it was pretty neat, being able to create your own content. I figured the bootcamp would be a great place to start on a career in it so I took the leap. I'm a hard worker and a fast learner. If i'm interested in something then it's hard for me to not jump into the deep end.

Currently I work as a bartender and server at a local japanese restaurant called Kabuki. I've worked there for almost nine years. It is really the only job I've had as an adult.
